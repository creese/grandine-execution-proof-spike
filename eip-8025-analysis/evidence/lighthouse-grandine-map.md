## Porting cautions

- Do not copy Lighthouse's proof-backed `payload_valid` mutation: it conflicts with the EIP's consensus-containment statements.
- Do not treat Lighthouse's size, root-only public input, quorum, memory retention, pre-Gloas lifecycle, or validation omissions as normative.
- Use Grandine's controller task/action/message, storage, and two-layer networking patterns rather than copying Lighthouse crate boundaries.
